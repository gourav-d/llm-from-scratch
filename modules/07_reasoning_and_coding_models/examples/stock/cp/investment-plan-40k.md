## 📋 PHASE 2.5: Smart Money Tracking — FII/DII & Bulk Deals

### Why Track Institutional Money?
```
"Follow the smart money" — When FIIs (Foreign Institutional Investors) and
DIIs (Domestic Institutional Investors like mutual funds, insurance companies)
are BUYING a stock, it's a strong signal of confidence. These institutions
have teams of analysts and billions of rupees — if they're accumulating,
there's usually a good reason.

Similarly, BULK DEALS (transactions > 0.5% of total shares on a single day)
often indicate that big players are entering or exiting a stock.
```

### What's Available on Screener.in?
Screener.in supports these institutional holding filters:
- `Change in FII holding` — Quarterly change in FII/FPI stake (%)
- `Change in DII holding` — Quarterly change in DII stake (%)
- `FII holding` — Current total FII/FPI stake (%)
- `DII holding` — Current total DII stake (%)
- `Change in Promoter holding` — Quarterly change in promoter stake
- `Number of FII` — Count of FII investors

> **Note:** Screener.in does NOT have a direct "bulk deals" filter in queries.
> For bulk/block deal data, use **Trendlyne** or **Tickertape** (see tools below).
