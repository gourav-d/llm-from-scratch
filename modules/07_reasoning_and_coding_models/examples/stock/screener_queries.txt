================================================================================
RISK MANAGEMENT RULES
================================================================================

1. NEVER put all money in one stock
2. SET stop-loss at 15% for long-term, 7-10% for swing trades
3. REBALANCE if any stock exceeds 40% of portfolio
4. REVIEW quarterly results - sell if fundamentals deteriorate
5. AVOID stocks with pledged promoter holdings
6. DON'T buy if you don't understand the business
7. KEEP 10-15% cash for averaging down or opportunities


================================================================================
SWING TRADING TIPS (For 20-30% of your Rs. 20,000)
================================================================================

1. Entry Signals:
   - Stock in uptrend but pulling back
   - RSI between 30-40 (oversold)
   - Positive MACD crossover
   - Breaking above resistance with volume

2. Exit Signals:
   - Hit 15-20% profit target
   - 7-10% stop-loss triggered
   - RSI above 70 (overbought)
   - Negative divergence

3. Position Sizing:
   - Max Rs. 5,000 per swing trade
   - Trade only 1-2 stocks at a time
   - Never average down on swing trades

4. Best Timeframe:
   - Hold 1 week to 3 months
   - Use daily charts for analysis
   - Avoid trading during results season


================================================================================
RED FLAGS - AVOID THESE STOCKS
================================================================================

1. Pledged promoter holding > 20%
2. Declining promoter holding for 2+ quarters
3. Debt to Equity > 3 (except banks)
4. Negative cash flow from operations
5. Auditor qualifications in annual report
6. Frequent related-party transactions
7. Promoter/management legal issues
8. Penny stocks < Rs. 10
9. Stocks with trading restrictions/T2T segment
10. Very low liquidity (avg. volume < 10,000)
