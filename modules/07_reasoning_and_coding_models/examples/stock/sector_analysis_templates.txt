HOW TO USE THESE TEMPLATES
================================================================================

STEP 1: Run screener query for desired sector
STEP 2: Shortlist 5-10 stocks from results
STEP 3: Fill out this template for each shortlisted stock
STEP 4: Compare filled templates side-by-side
STEP 5: Choose top 2-3 stocks with best metrics
STEP 6: Do final price chart analysis
STEP 7: Make purchase decision

RATING SYSTEM:
-------------
For each metric, assign score:
- Excellent: 3 points
- Good: 2 points
- Average: 1 point
- Poor: 0 points

Total Score > 25: Strong Buy
Total Score 20-25: Buy
Total Score 15-20: Hold/Watch
Total Score < 15: Avoid


SAVE THESE TEMPLATES:
--------------------
Create a folder for each stock you analyze.
Keep filled templates for future reference.
Update quarterly with new results.


Good luck with your sector analysis!
