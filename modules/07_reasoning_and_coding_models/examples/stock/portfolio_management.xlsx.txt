================================================================================
PORTFOLIO TRACKER - INSTRUCTIONS
================================================================================

Open stock_tracker.csv in Excel/Google Sheets and use these formulas:

COLUMN FORMULAS:
---------------

Column H (Current Value):
=G2*C2

Column I (Profit/Loss):
=H2-F2

Column J (Profit/Loss %):
=(I2/F2)*100

Column K (Days Held):
=TODAY()-C2


ADDITIONAL SHEETS TO CREATE:
----------------------------

SHEET 2: PORTFOLIO SUMMARY
--------------------------
Total Investment:           =SUM(stock_tracker!F:F)
Current Portfolio Value:    =SUM(stock_tracker!H:H)
Total Profit/Loss:          =SUM(stock_tracker!I:I)
Total Profit/Loss %:        =(Total_PL/Total_Investment)*100
Cash Available:             20000 - Total_Investment

Asset Allocation:
Conservative Stocks:        =SUMIF(stock_tracker!M:M,"Long-term",stock_tracker!H:H)
Swing Trading:              =SUMIF(stock_tracker!M:M,"Swing",stock_tracker!H:H)

Sector Diversification:
Banking:                    =SUMIF(stock_tracker!B:B,"Banking",stock_tracker!H:H)
IT:                         =SUMIF(stock_tracker!B:B,"IT",stock_tracker!H:H)
Pharma:                     =SUMIF(stock_tracker!B:B,"Pharma",stock_tracker!H:H)
[Add other sectors as needed]

================================================================================
COLOR CODING SYSTEM (Conditional Formatting)
================================================================================

Profit/Loss % (Column J):
- Green: > 10%
- Light Green: 0% to 10%
- Light Red: 0% to -10%
- Dark Red: < -10%

Status (Column N):
- Green: Active
- Blue: Monitoring (near target)
- Yellow: Warning (near stop-loss)
- Red: Sold

Days Held:
- Red: < 7 days (too early to judge)
- Yellow: 7-30 days
- Green: > 30 days (long-term holding)

================================================================================
RISK METRICS TO TRACK
================================================================================

1. Portfolio Concentration:
   Max allocation per stock: 25%
   Max allocation per sector: 40%

2. Volatility:
   Track max daily drop in portfolio value
   If > 10% in a day, review stop-losses

3. Cash Reserve:
   Always keep 10-15% in cash for opportunities

4. Win Rate:
   Closed Profitable Trades / Total Closed Trades
   Target: > 60%

5. Average Holding Period:
   Long-term: Should be > 6 months
   Swing: Should be 1-12 weeks

   - For averaging down opportunities


================================================================================
TAX PLANNING NOTES
================================================================================

Long-Term Capital Gains (LTCG):
- Holding period: > 12 months
- Tax: 12.5% above Rs. 1.25 lakh profit per year
- Strategy: Plan exit to optimize tax

Short-Term Capital Gains (STCG):
- Holding period: < 12 months
- Tax: 20%
- For swing trades, factor this into profit calculations

Dividend Income:
- Added to your income, taxed as per slab

Tip: Keep track of holding period for each stock
