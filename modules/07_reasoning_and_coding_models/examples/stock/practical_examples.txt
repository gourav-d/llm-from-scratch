EXAMPLE 1: FINDING A SAFE BANKING STOCK FOR LONG-TERM
================================================================================

STEP 1: RUN THE QUERY
--------------------
Go to screener.in → "Create New Screen"
Paste this query:

Market Capitalization > 1000 AND
Market Capitalization < 30000 AND
PE < 12 AND
Price to Book Value < 1.5 AND
Return on Assets > 1% AND
Gross NPA < 3% AND
Net NPA < 1% AND
Current Price < 52 Week High * 0.7 AND
Dividend Yield > 1.5% AND
Price > 50

Click "Run Screen"

STEP 2: ANALYZE RESULTS
----------------------
Let's say the query returns these stocks:
1. Federal Bank - Rs. 142
2. Karnataka Bank - Rs. 185
3. City Union Bank - Rs. 120
4. South Indian Bank - Rs. 18
5. RBL Bank - Rs. 156

STEP 3: ELIMINATE RED FLAGS
---------------------------
South Indian Bank: Price Rs. 18 (too low, risky) - ELIMINATE
RBL Bank: Check news - recent management issues - ELIMINATE

Shortlist: Federal Bank, Karnataka Bank, City Union Bank

STEP 4: DEEP DIVE (Use Banking Template)
----------------------------------------
Federal Bank:
- CASA Ratio: 34% (Good)
- Gross NPA: 1.8% (Excellent)
- Net NPA: 0.5% (Excellent)
- ROA: 1.3% (Good)
- Branch network: Strong in South India
- Digital initiatives: Good mobile app ratings
- Recent news: Clean, no issues
SCORE: 8/10

Karnataka Bank:
- CASA Ratio: 32% (Decent)
- Gross NPA: 2.9% (Acceptable)
- Net NPA: 0.9% (Good)
- ROA: 1.0% (Acceptable)
- Smaller network than Federal
SCORE: 6/10

City Union Bank:
- CASA Ratio: 28% (Average)
- Gross NPA: 2.5% (Good)
- Strong dividend history
- Conservative lending
SCORE: 7/10

STEP 5: TECHNICAL ANALYSIS
--------------------------
Federal Bank:
- 52W High: Rs. 210
- Current: Rs. 142 (32% down - good discount)
- Support visible at Rs. 135
- Good volume

Karnataka Bank:
- 52W High: Rs. 250
- Current: Rs. 185 (26% down)
- Lower volumes

STEP 6: DECISION
---------------
WINNER: Federal Bank
- Best fundamentals
- Good discount from high
- Strong CASA and low NPA
- Better volume for swing trades too

INVESTMENT PLAN:
- Allocate: Rs. 5,000
- Buy at: Rs. 142 or below Rs. 140
- Quantity: 35 shares (Rs. 4,900)
- Stop-loss: Rs. 120 (15% below)
- Target 1: Rs. 170 (20% profit)

EXAMPLE 5: DEALING WITH A FALLING STOCK
================================================================================

SCENARIO: You bought Federal Bank at Rs. 142 for Rs. 5,000
Stock falls to Rs. 128 (10% down)

PANIC RESPONSE (WRONG):
"Stock is falling! Everyone says market will crash! SELL NOW!"
Result: Book 10% loss, regret later when it bounces

CORRECT RESPONSE:
Step 1: CHECK STOP-LOSS
- Your stop-loss: Rs. 120
- Current price: Rs. 128
- Status: ABOVE stop-loss
Action: Don't panic sell

Step 2: CHECK FUNDAMENTALS
- Any bad news about Federal Bank? NO
- Quarterly results deteriorated? NO
- NPA increased? NO
- Management issue? NO
- Industry-wide fall? YES (all banks down 8-12%)

Step 3: CHECK REASONS FOR FALL
- RBI increased interest rates (temporary market reaction)
- Entire banking sector down
- Not specific to Federal Bank

Step 4: DECISION
This is MARKET CORRECTION, not stock-specific issue.

OPTIONS:
A. Hold and wait (if no fresh cash)
   - Keep stop-loss at Rs. 120
   - Review again next week

B. Average down (if have extra Rs. 2,000):
   - Buy 14 more shares at Rs. 128 = Rs. 1,792
   - New average: (142×35 + 128×14) ÷ 49 = Rs. 138
   - New stop-loss: Rs. 117 (15% below Rs. 138)
   - Lower average means faster recovery

CHOSEN ACTION: Average down with Rs. 1,792
- Total investment now: Rs. 6,762
- Average price: Rs. 138
- If stock goes to Rs. 165: Profit = 19.5% (vs 16% without averaging)

3 MONTHS LATER:
Federal Bank at Rs. 172
Without averaging: Profit = (172-142)/142 = 21%
With averaging: Profit = (172-138)/138 = 24.6%

Averaging down WORKED because fundamentals were strong!

Option A: Full rotation
- Sell all Federal Bank: 35 shares at Rs. 182 = Rs. 6,370

Option B: Partial rotation

KEY LEARNINGS FROM EXAMPLES
================================================================================

1. PATIENCE PAYS:
   Federal Bank: From Rs. 142 to Rs. 182 took 6 months
   Don't expect overnight riches

2. FUNDAMENTALS MATTER:
   Averaging down worked because fundamentals were strong
   Would have failed on weak stock

3. PARTIAL EXITS ARE SMART:
   Sell 50% at target, let rest run
   Protects profits while capturing upside

4. STOP-LOSS IS SACRED:
   If it hits, exit without emotions
   Saves you from bigger losses

5. QUARTERLY REVIEWS ARE CRITICAL:
   Results showed Federal was still strong
   Justified holding despite 28% gain

6. SECTOR ROTATION WORKS:
   Banking up 25% → Book some profits
   IT down 15% → Buy opportunity

7. DIVERSIFICATION SAVES:
   When auto fell, pharma protected portfolio
   No single stock decides your fate

8. CASH IS A POSITION:
   Keeping 5-10% cash helps grab opportunities
   Don't be 100% invested always
