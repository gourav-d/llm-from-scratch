QUESTIONS? TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Q: Market fell 5% today. Should I sell everything?
A: NO! Check if YOUR stocks' fundamentals changed. If not, HOLD.

Q: Stock fell 12%. Near my 15% stop-loss. What to do?
A: Check WHY it fell. If company-specific bad news, exit now.
   If market-wide fall, wait for stop-loss to actually trigger.

Q: Found a stock at Rs. 8 with good PE ratio. Buy?
A: NO! Avoid stocks below Rs. 20. Too risky (penny stocks).

Q: Should I buy more of a winning stock or losing stock?
A: Generally, add to winners (if not over-allocated).
   Only add to losers if fundamentals are still strong.

Q: How long to hold before selling?
A: Long-term: Minimum 1 year (ideally 3-5 years)
   Swing trade: 1 week to 3 months

Q: All my stocks are in profit. Book profit or hold?
A: Follow your target system. At 20% profit, book 50%.
   Let remaining 50% run towards 40% target.

Q: Can I use this for daily trading?
A: NO! These queries are for long-term investing.
   Daily trading requires different strategies (and is risky).
